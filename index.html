ABOUT US

Ultra Vision school at Surendranagar was established in the year 2007, and is affiliated to the Gujarat Secondary & Higher Secondary Board. Ultra Vision school is managed by Trimurti Education Trust, and was established in 1999. Since inception, the School has made its presence felt in the field of academic segment, especially 11th & 12th Science. Aiming for all round development of its students, the school provides best education and infrastructure. It aims at including the best of education and opportunities to excel in diverse fields.

The school trains its students to become citizens of high principles with a sympathetic and compassionate outlook.
